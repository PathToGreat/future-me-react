Pre-loaded Habits

Here are some example habits that can help shape your future:

Health

Drink 8 glasses of water daily

Staying properly hydrated improves energy levels, brain function, and helps maintain physical performance.

Mindfulness

5-minute daily meditation

Regular meditation reduces stress, improves concentration, and increases self-awareness.

Productivity

Plan tomorrow's tasks before bed

Evening planning reduces decision fatigue and helps you start your day with clarity and purpose.

Finance

Track daily expenses

Expense tracking creates awareness of spending patterns and helps identify areas to save money.

Relationships

Call a friend or family member

Regular connection with loved ones strengthens relationships and provides emotional support.
