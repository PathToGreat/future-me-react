Improve the core avatar experience based on the avatar-system audit.

Do not rewrite the avatar system. Do not change projection math, scoring logic, Firebase, Future Lab generation, Replicate, or Identity Trajectory Engine calculations.

This is a restrained UX and visual-mapping improvement pass for the deterministic SVG avatar experience.

Goal:
Make Current Me vs Future Me easier to understand and more emotionally meaningful while keeping the system honest, explainable, and consistent.

---

1. Strengthen Current Me vs Future Me contrast at the render layer only

The audit shows that projection math is intentionally conservative, but the visual difference between Current Me and Future Me can be too subtle for users.

Add a display-only visual delta amplification layer.

Requirements:

* Do not change trait scores
* Do not change projection calculations
* Do not change stored data
* Only amplify how the difference is rendered visually
* Keep the amplification bounded and believable

Suggested approach:

* compare Current Me avatar params to Future Me avatar params
* apply a modest multiplier to visible differences only
* cap the amplified values so the avatar never becomes exaggerated
* use this only in the side-by-side Current Me vs Future Me comparison

Suggested multiplier:

* start around 1.25x to 1.4x
* do not exceed 1.5x without clear reason

Apply carefully to:

* posture
* shoulder alignment
* facial brightness
* facial tension
* energy glow
* stance openness

Do not create extreme body changes.

---

2. Add visible channels for Social Connectedness and Purpose Alignment

The audit shows these traits currently have weak or nearly invisible visual influence.

Give each trait a clearer but still restrained visual expression.

Social Connectedness may influence:

* expression warmth
* eye softness
* slight openness of face
* subtle warmth in glow or aura

Purpose Alignment may influence:

* centeredness
* steadiness
* vertical alignment
* calmer visual focus
* subtle aura stability

Do not make these cartoonish.
Do not use religious symbols, badges, icons, or labels on the avatar body.

The effect should be felt visually but still grounded.

---

3. Improve facial expression range

Currently emotional stability mostly affects a single tension axis.

Expand the facial expression system slightly so the avatar can reflect states such as:

* tired
* tense
* calm
* bright
* open

Use existing trait inputs:

* emotionalStability
* vitality
* confidence
* socialConnectedness

This should remain subtle and human-like.

Do not add exaggerated smiles, frowns, or cartoon emotions.

---

4. Clarify avatar system labels in the UI

Add small, calm explanatory labels where helpful.

For the SVG/avatar screen:
Use language like:
“Trajectory Figure”
or
“Deterministic trajectory figure”

Short description:
“Built from your logged patterns and identity traits.”

For Future Lab:
Use language like:
“Experimental AI render”

Short description:
“An experimental visualization based on your trajectory. Results may vary.”

The purpose is to reduce confusion between:

* core deterministic avatar
* experimental AI image render

Do not make this legal-heavy. Keep it simple.

---

5. Improve Visual Influences explanation

Update VisualInfluences so it better explains what is affecting the avatar.

Include:

* posture and energy influences
* habit rhythm influence
* social/relational influence if active
* purpose/alignment influence if active

Keep the tone observational.

Example:
“Recent movement and sleep patterns are supporting energy and posture.”
“Connection-related inputs are softening expression and openness.”
“Purpose-related inputs are contributing to steadiness and centeredness.”

Do not say:

* “Your avatar improved”
* “You earned”
* “You unlocked”

---

6. Acceptance criteria

* Current Me vs Future Me is visibly clearer without changing underlying projection math
* Social Connectedness and Purpose Alignment have visible but restrained influence
* Facial expression feels more emotionally meaningful
* Users can better understand why the avatar looks the way it does
* SVG/avatar system and Future Lab AI render are clearly distinguished
* No scoring, projection, Firebase, or Replicate logic is changed
* No exaggerated or gamified visual effects are added
