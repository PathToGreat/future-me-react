Phase AA: Clarity, Trust, and Sharability Layer
Build the following components without changing any existing logic, scoring systems, or avatar calculations.
1. “What This Means” Clarity Panel
Add a small expandable panel on the Home screen beneath key insights that explains outcomes in plain language.
Requirements:
Translates observed changes into simple cause-effect language
Example tone:
“Your stress trend stabilized this week. This usually happens when sleep timing becomes more consistent.”
No advice
No instructions
No calls to action
Collapsed by default
Optional to open
Purpose:
Help users understand why something changed without telling them what to do.
2. Shareable Progress Explanation Card
Extend ProgressSnapshot with a second optional view called “What Changed”.
Requirements:
One short paragraph explaining the most meaningful shift since baseline
Uses neutral, descriptive language
No percentages unless already present
Example tone:
“Since starting, sleep consistency increased and stress variability decreased. This pattern typically supports steadier energy.”
Purpose:
Allows users to share progress with a spouse, friend, coach, or investor without oversharing data.
3. Trust & Transparency Micro-Copy
Add subtle informational copy in three locations:
A. Avatar Screen
One line under the avatar:
“Your baseline only updates through reassessment, not daily logs.”
B. Insights Screen
One line at the bottom:
“Insights reflect patterns, not diagnoses or predictions.”
C. Devices Screen
Clarify status:
“Device connections are prepared but not yet active. When available, they will enhance tracking without changing your baseline.”
Purpose:
Reinforces honesty, integrity, and expectations without disclaimers.
4. “How People Use This” Lightweight Social Proof
Add a rotating single-sentence card on the Home screen.
Requirements:
Not testimonials
No names
No hype
Examples:
“Some users notice changes in stress patterns within the first two weeks.”
“Many people use this as a daily awareness check, not a habit streak.”
Purpose:
Normalizes use and reduces performance pressure.
5. Founder Signal: Clarity Confidence Score (Internal Only)
Add an internal metric that tracks:
How often users expand “What This Means”
How often ProgressSnapshot is viewed or shared
Whether users return after viewing an explanation
Purpose:
Measures whether clarity increases retention without pushing behavior.
