1. Baseline Physical State
Not physique measurements, not weight, not anything sensitive.
Just simple indicators of wellness that give “Current Me” a believable anchor.
Add:
Energy level most days
(1–5 scale)
Resting fatigue
“Do you wake up tired even after a full night’s sleep?”
Yes / No / Sometimes
General body tension
(Neck, shoulders, gut tightness)
1–5 scale
These three help shape:
posture baseline
facial expression baseline
under-eye shading baseline
energy aura baseline
2. Lifestyle Rhythm
Not habits. Rhythm.
Add:
Daily movement type:
Light / Moderate / Intense
(not duration, just type)
Eating rhythm:
Regular meals / irregular / frequent snacking
Sleep rhythm:
Consistent bedtime / inconsistent / very irregular
These help the avatar understand:
“Why” a user is the way they are
What will likely change first
Where to apply future projections
3. Emotional State + Stressors
This affects face, posture, energy, and long-term projections.
Add:
Primary stress source:
Options:
Work / Family / Money / Uncertainty / Health / Other / None
Overall emotional climate:
Overwhelmed / Neutral / Hopeful
Social support:
Strong / Average / Low
These three are extremely valuable because:
low social support = interpretation of stress changes
overwhelmed baseline means “Current Me” starts more fatigued
hopeful baseline boosts positive glow
4. Faith + Purpose + Motivation
This is unique to your brand and gives a deeper picture of what drives the user’s transformation.
Add:
Meaning/purpose direction:
“Do you feel aligned with your purpose right now?”
Aligned / Searching / Disconnected
Faith practice rhythm:
Consistent / inconsistent / not currently practicing
Internal motivation:
“How motivated are you to change?”
1–5 scale
These give:
subtle glow intensification
clarity filter strength
future projection optimism scaling
more meaningful suggestions later
What NOT to add
Do not add:
Weight
Height
Body fat
Clothing sizes
Photos inside the assessment
Anything medical
Too many sliders
Long text answers
Questions that shame the user
Anything that takes more than 2 minutes
You want behavioral indicators, not biological measurements.
The updated flow should look like:
Section 1: Core Habits (current 4 sliders)
Physical activity

Sleep quality
Stress level

Energy level
Morning fatigue
Body tension
Section 3: Lifestyle Rhythm
Movement rhythm
Eating rhythm
Sleep rhythm

Primary stress source
Emotional climate

Section 5: Faith + Purpose + Motivation
Purpose alignment
Faith rhythm
