### 1. Strengthen contrast in key areas

Right now most surfaces are too close to white.

* Increase saturation slightly on primary and reflection surfaces
* Reduce how quickly gradients fade to pure white
* Maintain softness, but avoid washed-out appearance

Goal:
At a glance, the user should immediately see:
Top zone → Reflection → Secondary info

* soft gradient background (very subtle)
* rounded-3xl (slightly larger than cards)

* internal padding (p-3 or p-4)

Inside that container:

* keep the tighter spacing between the two elements
* remove the feeling of two separate cards

This should feel like:
“One active system panel”

### 3. Create a visual “pause” before the CTA

Right now the CTA flows directly after content.

Introduce a subtle separation:

* add slightly increased margin-top before CTA

* give the CTA container a slightly stronger gradient than surrounding elements

Goal:
The CTA should feel like an intentional action point, not just the next item in a list
