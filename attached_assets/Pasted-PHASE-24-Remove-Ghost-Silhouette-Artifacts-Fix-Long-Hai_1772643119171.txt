PHASE 24: Remove Ghost Silhouette Artifacts + Fix Long Hair Face Occlusion

Two issues remain in Avatar V2:

1) Background ghost silhouette shapes and faint lines still appear behind the avatar.
   It looks like a body outline or duplicate shape behind the main figure.

2) Long hair currently covers the whole face area and reads like a mask (Chewbacca effect).
   Hair must never cover eyes/eyebrows/mouth.

Do NOT redesign the screen.
Do NOT change projection logic.
This phase is strictly SVG render cleanup and hair layering correctness.

-----------------------------------------
A) ELIMINATE GHOST SILHOUETTE / BACKGROUND ARTIFACTS
-----------------------------------------

Requirement:
There must be only one visible body silhouette.

Implement:
1) Audit HumanAvatarRenderer for any duplicate body shapes used for:
   - clipPaths
   - masks
   - shading alignment
   - filter targets
   - debug outlines

2) Ensure any helper paths used for clip/mask purposes are truly non-rendering:
   - They must not paint fill or stroke in the visible output.

3) Remove or disable any remaining background silhouette layer, guide stroke, or low-opacity path that could read as a ghost body.

Definition of done:
On a plain background, there are no faint body outlines, no stray lines, and no duplicate silhouette behind the avatar.

-----------------------------------------
B) FIX LONG HAIR SO FACE STAYS CLEAR
-----------------------------------------

Requirement:
Long hair must read as long hair without covering the face.

Rules:
- Hair can frame the face, but cannot cover:
  eyes, eyebrows, nose, mouth
- Hair must have a visible hairline.
- Hair must sit behind face features.
- Hair must remain aligned across headScale and postureLean.

Implement:
1) Update the "long" hair style so it includes a face opening.
2) Ensure hair is layered behind the face feature layer.
3) Ensure hair does not overlap facial area even at extreme headScale or posture settings.

Definition of done:
Long hair looks human and the face remains fully visible and readable.
