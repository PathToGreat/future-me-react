Phase Y – Evidence of Value & Signal Clarity
Objective: Strengthen user belief that the app is working by making progress more obvious, attributable, and meaningful while quietly preparing the product for investor, advisor, and partner conversations.
1. “Why This Changed” Layer (Signal Attribution)
Add a lightweight explanation layer that helps users understand why something improved or declined.
What to build:
* When a metric, insight, or Focus Zone updates, include a short contextual explanation such as:
    * “This shift appears connected to 4 days of consistent sleep timing.”
    * “Stress stabilized during a week with fewer skipped logs.”
* These explanations should be:
    * Descriptive, not corrective
    * Based only on patterns already tracked
    * Shown sparingly, not everywhere

2. Personal Proof Moments (Quiet Wins)
Introduce moments where users see evidence of progress without celebration language.
What to build:
* A small “Noticing” or “Observed Change” card that appears occasionally (not daily)
* Examples:
    * “Your baseline stress is now lower than when you started.”
    * “You are logging with more consistency than your first two weeks.”
* These should:
    * Never say “congrats” or “great job”
    * Never use scores or gamification
    * Appear only when change is statistically meaningful


3. Lightweight Outcome Summary (Shareable, Optional)
Prepare the app for organic sharing without pushing it.
What to build:
* A simple, optional “Progress Snapshot” view that shows:
    * Starting point vs current averages
    * One Focus Zone insight
    * One reflection-style observation
* This view should be:
    * Private by default
    * Easily shareable if the user chooses
    * Framed as “Here’s what I’m noticing about myself”


4. Readiness for Real Data (Without Turning It On)
Do not add new integrations yet.
What to build:
* Add clear language in the Devices area that explains:
    * What device data will enhance
    * What it will not change (baseline integrity)
* Include a passive indicator like:
    * “Designed to support future passive data when available”

5. Founder Signal Pack (Internal Only)
Quietly prepare yourself for smarter conversations.
What to build:
* Expand the hidden Founder Metrics Dashboard to include:
    * Average days to first “noticed change”
    * % of users who reach 7-day consistency
    * % who return after first insight
* No graphs needed
* Just clean, readable signals
