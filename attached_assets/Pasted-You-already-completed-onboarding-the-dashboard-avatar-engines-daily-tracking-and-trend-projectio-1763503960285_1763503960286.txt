Health: Existing wellness score plus sleep, activity, and nutrition trend direction
Social Emotional: Stress values, logging consistency, and stress trend slope
Wealth: Logging consistency multiplied by a fixed factor, capped at a maximum
Faith: Daily consistency with bonus for streaks
Family: Combinations of stress and sleep trend directions
Community: Consistency and average stress values

All zones must output both a score and a details object
