(Phase 5: Micro-Suggestions Engine)
Objective:
Build a Micro-Suggestions Engine for the Future Me app that provides concise, actionable, and insight-driven guidance after daily log submissions. Language should align with conscious discipline and PTG-style communication: neutral, non-judgmental, factual, and encouraging reflection and adjustment without value labeling or “good/bad” language.
Requirements:
Integration:
Micro-suggestions appear immediately after a daily log submission.
Suggestions are visible on the Dashboard as a short message (1–2 lines).
Users can expand for more details or view weekly/monthly insights for context.
Logic & Triggers:
Compare current log metrics to:
Baseline (from onboarding/reassessment)
7-day moving average
Identify areas of improvement or patterns, focusing on small, actionable steps.
Suggestions should adapt based on combined metrics, e.g., sleep + activity, nutrition + hydration.
Life Zones Covered:
Physical Activity
Nutrition
Sleep
Stress / Emotional Wellness
Faith / Purpose / Motivation
Combined metrics insights
Behavior & Tone Rules:
Neutral and descriptive language: focus on patterns, behaviors, and outcomes.
Avoid value judgments (“good/bad job”)
Avoid condescending or patronizing phrasing
Keep short, actionable, reflective suggestions
Encourage small, achievable changes
Maintain PTG conscious discipline tone: clarity, observation, empowerment
Example Micro-Suggestions:
Physical Activity / Fitness:
“Steps are consistent this week. Adding a brief walk after lunch can support daily rhythm.”
“Movement today was moderate. Consider a light stretch before bed to improve recovery.”
Nutrition:
“Vegetable intake is steady. Adding a protein source to breakfast may balance energy levels.”
“Hydration levels are lower than baseline. Drinking water before meals can support focus and recovery.”
Sleep:
“Sleep duration has been variable. Maintaining a consistent bedtime for the next 3 nights can stabilize energy.”
“Rest was shorter than baseline. A brief pre-sleep routine may improve sleep onset.”
Stress / Emotional Wellness:
“Stress indicators increased today. A 5-minute breathing exercise can support clarity and calm.”
“Evening mood trends slightly low. Journaling for reflection may provide perspective.”
Faith / Purpose / Motivation:
“Motivation score is steady. Setting one clear intention for tomorrow may strengthen follow-through.”
“Purpose alignment is marked as searching. Reflecting on one meaningful goal today can guide next steps.”
Combined Metrics / Patterns:
“Energy is low and sleep was inconsistent. Prioritizing an earlier bedtime could improve alertness tomorrow.”
“Balanced nutrition and movement are trending well. Consider a brief meditation to support emotional clarity.”
Implementation Notes:
Each micro-suggestion should be generated based on current log metrics + baseline trends.
Suggestions should be modular, allowing for easy expansion or adjustment by metric type.
Ensure suggestions do not modify avatar calculations—Current Me remains baseline-anchored; Future Me uses trends for projections.
