PHASE 20: Body Model Integrity & Cross-Gender Robustness

Context:
Avatar V2 is live and Phase 19 is adding projection + photo overlays.
Before moving further, we must harden the body modeling system to ensure:

1) Preset interpolation is mathematically correct and intuitive.
2) Muscular, lean, soft, and overweight bodies are clearly distinguishable.
3) Male and female bodies can represent all body types without distortion.
4) Emotional traits do not unintentionally drive physical composition.
5) No preset blending artifacts occur at tier boundaries.

This phase is strictly structural refinement of body modeling.

-----------------------------------------
A) FIX PRESET INTERPOLATION LOGIC
-----------------------------------------

Current issue:
Preset neighbor selection and interpolation appear non-linear and may blend toward incorrect tiers.

Requirement:
Replace neighborScore-based logic with a clean tier-based interpolation model.

Implement:
1) Define ordered tiers:
   Overweight → Soft → AverageFit → LeanAthletic → MuscularAthletic

2) Determine which two tiers the composite physical score falls between.
3) Compute interpolation factor linearly within that band.
4) Blend ONLY between adjacent tiers.
5) Ensure full range 0–100 produces smooth transitions.

Result:
- No bouncing between distant presets.
- Smooth proportional change.
- Athletic bodies clearly emerge at high scores.

-----------------------------------------
B) SEPARATE PHYSICAL COMPOSITION FROM EMOTIONAL TRAITS
-----------------------------------------

Current risk:
Composite score includes emotional stability, resilience, etc.
This can incorrectly influence body mass or muscularity.

Requirement:
Create a dedicated PhysicalCompositionScore separate from IdentityCompositeScore.

PhysicalCompositionScore should be derived from:
- Activity
- Nutrition
- Consistency
- Sleep (minor influence)
- Optional: body assessment inputs if available

Emotional traits should influence:
- postureLean
- facialTension
- vibrancy
- energyGlow

But NOT:
- shoulderWidth
- chestSize
- armThickness
- legThickness
- waistTaper
- hipWidth

Result:
A disciplined but sedentary person does not appear muscular.
A resilient but inactive person does not gain taper.

-----------------------------------------
C) ENSURE ALL BODY TYPES WORK FOR BOTH MALE AND FEMALE
-----------------------------------------

Current risk:
Female adjustments always reduce shoulders and arms.
This can distort muscular female presets.

Requirement:
1) Allow every preset (including MuscularAthletic) to apply to both genders fully.
2) Female adjustments must preserve muscular potential.
3) Gender should adjust proportional distribution, not suppress strength.

Rules:
- Female muscular preset must still look athletic and strong.
- Overweight male and overweight female must both render realistically.
- Waist taper logic must respect both masculine and feminine shapes without caricature.

-----------------------------------------
D) ADD BODY VALIDATION TEST MATRIX
-----------------------------------------

Implement a simple internal validation function:

validateBodyModel()

It should simulate:
- All 5 presets
- Both genders
- Extreme physical scores (0, 25, 50, 75, 100)
- High emotional stability vs low emotional stability

Ensure:
- No broken geometry
- No inverted shapes
- No overlapping limbs
- No unnatural distortion
- No arms disconnected from torso
- No hips exceeding shoulder constraints unnaturally

This does not require visual screenshots.
Just ensure parameter outputs remain valid and bounded.

-----------------------------------------
E) LOCK BODY GEOMETRY CONSTRAINTS
-----------------------------------------

Add hard internal constraints:

- Shoulders must always be wider than neck.
- Waist cannot exceed hipWidth + defined buffer.
- Arms must scale proportionally with chestSize.
- Legs must scale proportionally with hipWidth.
- HeadScale must not exceed anatomical realism.

Ensure all geometry math clamps inside realistic human bounds.

-----------------------------------------
F) OUTPUT SUMMARY AFTER BUILD
-----------------------------------------

Return:
- Updated interpolation approach explanation
- How PhysicalCompositionScore is computed
- How gender adjustments now behave
- Any constraints added
- Confirmation that male and female muscular bodies both render correctly

-----------------------------------------
SUCCESS CRITERIA
-----------------------------------------

1) MuscularAthletic at 90–100 score clearly looks muscular for both genders.
2) LeanAthletic clearly differs from MuscularAthletic.
3) Overweight does not look cartoonish.
4) Emotional state changes face/posture but does NOT distort body mass.
5) No visual glitches across full score range.
6) No regressions in existing Avatar V2 wiring.

This phase ensures the avatar system is structurally correct for all body types before further identity projection enhancements.
