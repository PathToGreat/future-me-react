Implement Phase 9: Multi-Zone Visual Integration Layer for the Avatar.

Goal:
Expand the avatar engine so it can reflect influence from multiple life zones beyond Health, without adding visual clutter or symbolic overlays.

Important:
This must remain subtle and clean. No icons, no floating symbols, no exaggerated animations.

Step 1: Define Zones
Ensure the system can support these zones:
- Health (existing)
- Wealth
- Faith
- Family
- Community
- SocialEmotional

If some zones do not yet have full data tracking, scaffold them in a neutral way so future data can influence avatar traits.

Step 2: Influence Mapping Layer
Create a new module:
zoneInfluenceEngine.js

This layer should:
- Take zone-level scores or stability indicators
- Convert them into subtle avatar trait modifiers

Examples of acceptable subtle influences:

Wealth:
- Slight grounding posture shift when stable
- Slight forward tension when unstable

SocialEmotional:
- Micro facial tension vs softness
- Slight shoulder lift/drop

Family/Community:
- Subtle openness in torso posture vs contraction

Faith:
- Stability indicator reflected in reduced micro-variance of posture

Health:
- Remains primary driver of energy color and physical tone

Step 3: Integration
Modify avatar rendering pipeline to:
- First compute health-based traits (existing system)
- Then pass through zoneInfluenceEngine to apply subtle trait offsets
- Ensure changes are small enough that they do not distort the avatar

Step 4: Constraints
- No new colors introduced
- No symbolic graphics
- No labels on avatar itself
- No exaggerated movement
- If zone data is insufficient, do not fabricate signals

Step 5: Performance
- No heavy rendering changes
- Maintain SVG-based system
- No WebGL or 3D

Step 6: Transparency
In the Avatar screen (not Home), add a subtle expandable section:
“Visual Influences”
When expanded, show:
- Health: influencing posture + tone
- SocialEmotional: influencing expression
- etc.
Short descriptive text only. No scores displayed.

Do NOT:
- Change scoring logic
- Alter DirectionIndicator
- Modify Snapshot logic
- Add gamification

Outcome:
The avatar should feel deeper and more life-integrated, without becoming visually busy.

It should still look clean and intentional.
